# CVUT Guessr Privacy policy

**Last updated:** April 9, 2026

## Overview
This app is designed for casual use and respects your privacy. We do not collect, track, or sell any personal data.

## Data Collection
We do **not** collect or track any personal information automatically.

## Account Information
- You may create an account using your email and password.
- This information is only used to allow you to log in.
- You can delete your account at any time, and all associated data will be permanently removed.
- The reccomended method of deletion is by selecting delete account **in the app**.
- If you prefer, you can also email us at ohraband@cvut.cz to request account deletion. This process is **manual** and may take longer than deleting in the app.

## Photos
- You can upload photos to the app.
- You retain full ownership of any photos you upload.
- All uploaded photos are manually reviewed before being added to the map.
- Uploaded photos are **not tied to your user account**. If you wish to remove a photo, please contact us at ohraband@cvut.cz and provide information about which photo you want deleted.

## Purchases
- The app does not include any purchases, subscriptions, or payments.

## Data Sharing
- We do not share your data with third parties.

## Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your account information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ohraband@cvut.cz.
